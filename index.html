// The Origin

Built from nothing, for everyone.

"I had no laptop, no money, just a 5-year-old phone and a determination to build something real."

ProStudy OS was built entirely on a Samsung Galaxy A12 — a phone bought second-hand in Malaysia by a father who still uses a ₹1500 device himself. No laptop. No budget. Just Claude AI for planning, AI Studio for code generation, and relentless iteration.

From a small village in Chulachuli 5 ilam, Nepal, this app exists to prove that geography and resources don't limit what you can create.

// Capabilities

Everything a serious student needs.

📝
Rich Document Editor

Notion-style editor with headings, bold, italic, code blocks, blockquotes, and slash commands. Your thoughts, beautifully structured.

🧠
Active Recall Flashcards

Create Q&A cards inside any note. Review them in deck mode with tap-to-reveal. Science-backed spaced repetition for deeper memory.

🔍
OCR — Text in Photos

Scan physical notes with your camera. Tesseract.js extracts text from images so your handwritten notes become fully searchable.

🎙️
Voice Memos

Record audio notes directly inside any document. Attach voice explanations to complex topics for later review.

🏗️
Hierarchical Nodes

Organize knowledge like a tree. Create unlimited sub-nodes inside any document — like folders inside folders, infinitely deep.

🔒
PIN-Protected Vault

Lock your entire library with a 4-digit PIN. Everything stays private on your device — no cloud, no server, no compromise.
